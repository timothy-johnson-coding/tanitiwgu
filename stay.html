Booking tips

Stay smart on Taniti

📅

Book ahead in dry season

May–October is peak. Reserve resorts 2–3 months early for the best rooms.

💚

Stay local, give back

Guesthouses put your money straight into the island community.

🎒

Mix it up

Many guests split their trip: a few nights of jungle, a few on the beach.
