Welcome to Taniti

A small island with an enormous heart

Tucked into the South Pacific, Taniti spans less than 500 square miles yet packs in sandy and rocky beaches, a safe natural harbor, dense tropical rainforest, and a mountainous interior crowned by the active volcano Mount Pulu.

The island's 20,000 residents share a proud heritage and a relaxed pace of life. English is widely spoken alongside the native Merean language, and you'll never be far from a friendly face — or a freshly cracked coconut.

  • Safe, walkable capital city of Yasawa with a bustling harbor
  • Eco-tours, snorkeling, surfing and rainforest hikes
  • Modern hospital with multilingual staff, available 24/7
