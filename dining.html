Island cuisine

Flavours of the Pacific

Taniti's food is as fresh as it gets — fish landed that morning, fruit picked from the garden, and coconut in everything from curries to desserts. Traditional Merean cooking centres on the umu, an underground earth oven that slow-roasts pork, root vegetables and fish wrapped in banana leaves.

  • Kokoda — fresh fish "cooked" in citrus and coconut cream
  • Umu feast — earth-oven pork, taro and breadfruit
  • Tropical fruit — mango, papaya, passionfruit, fresh coconut
  • Reef-to-table seafood — grilled snapper, lobster, octopus
